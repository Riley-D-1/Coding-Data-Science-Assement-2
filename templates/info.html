Why is Cryptocurrency called dangerous?

Cryptocurrency isn't backed by the governemnts and the money held in crypto accounts are not insured by the government. If something happens to your cryptowallet there isn't much you can do. Furthermore Cryptocurrency is often linked to scams, with victims of crypto scams losing over $3.94 billion in 2023. According to the ACCC, crypto investment scams were 78% of all investment scams. Payments are usually not reversible, the payments don't come with legal protection. Cryptocurrency doesn't rely on banks to vertify transaction which sometimes can lead to crimes.
